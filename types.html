Types of DC Motors

1. Brushed DC Motor

Brushed DC motors use brushes to deliver current to the rotor. They are simple, cost-effective, and suitable for low-power applications.

2. Brushless DC Motor (BLDC)

Brushless DC motors use electronic commutation instead of brushes. They are more efficient, have longer life, and require less maintenance.

3. Permanent Magnet DC Motor (PMDC)

PMDC motors use permanent magnets to create the magnetic field. They are compact, efficient, and widely used in small devices.

4. Series and Shunt DC Motors

Series DC motors have high starting torque, suitable for traction systems. Shunt DC motors provide stable speed, ideal for industrial applications.
