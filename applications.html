Applications of DC Motors

Robotics

DC motors are widely used in robots for precise motion control, speed regulation, and torque adjustment.

Electric Vehicles

They are essential in electric vehicles for propulsion, providing smooth acceleration and efficient energy use.

Home Appliances

DC motors power devices like fans, mixers, and vacuum cleaners due to their reliability and control.

Industrial Machinery

DC motors are used in conveyor systems, elevators, and automated machines requiring precise speed and torque control.
