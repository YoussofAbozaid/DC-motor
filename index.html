What is a DC Motor?

A DC motor is an electric device that converts direct current (DC) electrical energy into mechanical motion. It is widely used in robotics, vehicles, and home appliances due to its simplicity and precise control.

Importance of DC Motors

DC motors are essential wherever accurate speed and torque control is required. They are key in automation, electric vehicles, and industrial machines. Learning about them enhances understanding of modern technology.

Key Features

  • Robust and simple design
  • High starting torque
  • Precise speed control
  • Wide applications
  • Reliable performance
